## Overview

NIVESH AI is a comprehensive GenAI and Agentic AI-powered financial analysis platform designed to simplify market analysis for everyday investors. The platform secured **2nd Position at Finvasia Innovation Hackathon 2026**.

## Key Features

- **Agentic AI Architecture**: Built intelligent AI agents that autonomously research, analyze, and present financial insights to users.
- **Multi-Asset Support**: Integrated tracking and analysis for stocks, mutual funds, and cryptocurrencies in a unified dashboard.
- **Multilingual AI Assistant**: Developed a multilingual AI-powered financial assistant that breaks language barriers in financial literacy.
- **Simplified Market Analysis**: Created intuitive data visualizations and AI-generated summaries to make complex market data accessible to beginners.
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Live market data integration with AI-powered trend analysis and predictions.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: Server-side rendered React application for optimal performance.
- **Node.js & Express.js**: Backend API development and data processing.
- **MongoDB**: Database for user portfolios and financial data.
- **LangChain**: Orchestration of AI agents and tool-calling capabilities.
- **Gemini API**: Generative AI for financial analysis and natural language processing.

## Achievements

- 🥈 **2nd Position** — Finvasia Innovation Hackathon 2026

## Impact

NIVESH AI democratizes financial analysis by leveraging generative AI to make complex market data accessible and actionable for users of all experience levels, regardless of their language or financial background.
